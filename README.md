# 🕊️ Weekly Spiritual Programme

> *"See, I am doing a new thing! Now it springs up; do you not perceive it?"*
> — Isaiah 43:19

A weekly, self-contained devotional programme published as a single HTML file. Each edition walks through three interlocking spiritual themes — **Fresh Starts**, **Confidence & Strength**, and **Peace & Guidance** — with scripture, theological revelation, daily prayer, and practical exercises across all seven days.

---

## About This Project

This repository is a living devotional resource. A new programme is generated each week and committed here, so that anyone who finds it can open any edition in a browser and walk through a structured week of Christian spiritual formation — with no app, no login, and no internet connection required.

Each weekly file is fully self-contained: all styling, navigation, and content is embedded in a single `.html` file.

---

## How to Use

**Read in your browser:**
Download any weekly file and open it directly in your browser. No server or setup needed.

**Navigate by day:**
Use the sidebar (desktop) or the tab bar (mobile) to move between days. You can also use the Previous / Next buttons at the bottom of each day's content.

**Print or save as PDF:**
Use your browser's print function (`Ctrl+P` / `Cmd+P`) and choose "Save as PDF" to keep a printable copy for the week.

## Weekly Themes

Each edition is structured around three complementary themes, each carried across approximately two days, with Sunday serving as a culminating reflection and sending prayer.

| Days | Theme | Anchor Scripture |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Mon – Tue | Fresh Starts | Isaiah 43:19 |
| Wed – Thu | Confidence & Strength | Isaiah 40:31 |
| Fri – Sun | Peace & Guidance | Proverbs 3:5–6 |

Themes may change from week to week. The structure — seven days, scripture, revelation, prayer, daily practices — remains consistent.

---

## What Each Day Contains

Every day in the programme includes four elements:

**Scripture** — the anchor verse for the day, presented prominently for meditation.

**Revelation** — a deeper theological engagement with the text. Not a surface reading, but a conversation with the passage: its original language, its context, and what it means for your life today.

**Morning Prayer** — a written prayer you can pray word-for-word, adapt, or use as a launching point for your own conversation with God.

**Daily Practices** — four concrete actions to carry the theme from reading into lived experience.

---

## Spiritual Foundations

This programme is rooted in orthodox Christian faith, drawing from:

- **Scripture** as the living Word of God (2 Timothy 3:16)
- **Prayer** as conversation with the Father through Christ (John 16:23–24)
- **The Holy Spirit** as the guide into all truth (John 16:13)
- **Community** — you are encouraged to share each week's insights with a trusted brother or sister in faith

It is non-denominational in presentation, but unapologetically Christ-centred in foundation.
